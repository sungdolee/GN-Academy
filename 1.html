GN Academy

Good Neighbors Global Capacity Development Center (GCDC) was established in 2017, to explicitly focus on the capacity development of staff in Good Neighbors Partner Countries.

GCDC is committed to building the competencies of GN Staff all around the world by offering a diverse range of training opportunities from hands-on, practical training on technical skiils needed for field implementation of community development programs to supporting organizational development such as DME, Local governance, Country strategic planning, and financial managment.

GCDC seeks to bring the best training resources to GN staff by learning and sharing the knowledge gained from direct, field experience of subject matter experts from both within and outside of GN. By fully equipping our staff to produce quality results, we believe GCDC contributes significantly to the fulfillment of GN's Mission
